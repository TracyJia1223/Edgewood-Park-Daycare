Why Families Choose Us

Every detail is designed with your little one in mind — from the toys we choose to the activities we plan.

Small Group Setting

With a maximum of 8 children, every child receives individualized attention, care, and love every single day.

Bilingual Environment

Your Child's Safety & Our Credentials

We hold ourselves to the highest standards — because your trust is something we earn every single day.

Licensed by BC MCFD

Fully licensed through BC's Ministry of Children and Family Development. All licensing requirements are met and regularly inspected.

First Aid & Infant CPR

All caregivers hold current Standard First Aid and Infant CPR certification — renewed regularly so we're always prepared.

Criminal Record Checks

Every adult caregiver has passed a complete criminal record check and vulnerable sector screening — no exceptions.

Secure Home Environment

Safety-proofed rooms, locked cabinets, gated stairways, and controlled entry — only authorized adults are admitted.

Emergency Protocols

Written emergency plans are always in place. Parents are notified immediately of any incident — full transparency, always.

Daily Health Check-ins

Every child receives a gentle health assessment at arrival. If a child seems unwell, we communicate with parents right away.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have openings available?
+

We maintain a small, intimate group of up to 8 children to ensure personalized care. Spots are limited and fill quickly! Please reach out via our contact form to check current availability and get on our waitlist.

How do I schedule a visit or tour?
+

We'd love to meet you and your little one! Simply fill out our contact form below or give us a call at (778) 319-6866. We'll arrange a time that works for your family to come see our space and meet the teachers.

What ages do you accept?
+

We lovingly care for infants and toddlers from 0 to 36 months (3 years) of age. Our program is specifically designed to support the unique developmental needs of babies and young toddlers.
